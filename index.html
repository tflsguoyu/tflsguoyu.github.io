I am a first year Ph.D student in the Department of Computer Science at University of California, Irvine, supervised by ZHAO Shuang. My interests include CG and CV, especially in geometry and rendering.

Previously, I was working as a Research Associate in BeingThere Centre at Nanyang Technological University (Singapore), with Pierre-Yves Laffont (ETH Zürich) and Tobias Martin (ETH Zürich), under supervision of CHAM Tat-Jen.

I obtained my M.S. degree from Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (Shenzhen, China), advised by HENG Pheng-Ann. And B.S. degree from Central South University (Changsha, China).
