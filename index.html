Curriculum Vitæ (pdf)

I am currently working as a Research Associate in Institute for Media Innovation at Nanyang Technological University (Singapore). My advisor is Prof. Tat-Jen Cham and I am working with Dr. Pierre-Yves Laffont (ETH Zürich) and Dr. Tobias Martin (ETH Zürich). My work focuses on 3D based video manipulating.

I obtained my M.S. degree from Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology (Shenzhen, China) in 2013. I did some work on physically-based soft body simulation. Before that, I got my B.S. degree of Mathematics in Central South University (Changsha, China) in 2010. During my undergraduate study, I was interested in Fractal Theory.

Now my interests include graphics and vision, especially in geometry and virtual reality.
